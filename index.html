Arcade Frogger Game - Gem Digger

project for Udacity Front-End Nanodegree

How to play

  • Use LEFT and RIGHT to select character. Press ENTER to start game.
  • Use LEFT, RIGHT, UP and DOWN to move character.
  • Collect GEMs as many as you can.
  • Once hit bug, game over. Press SPACE to restart game.
  • Eat STAR to recover life.
